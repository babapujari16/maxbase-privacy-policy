MaxBase Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

1. Data Collection

MaxBase does not collect or transmit any personal information to servers or third parties. We store only:

  • In-game player tags and usernames
  • App preferences such as theme, notification settings
  • Locally saved data using your device's storage

2. Local Storage

All app data is stored securely on your device. You can delete this data anytime from within the app or your device's storage settings.

3. No Tracking or Sharing

This app does not track user behavior, does not share your data with anyone, and does not include third-party analytics or ads.

4. Data Export and Screenshots

Users can export saved account data or share app screenshots. These features are entirely local and no files are sent to any server.

5. Settings and Preferences

Your settings (theme, notification sound, etc.) are saved locally. You can reset or modify them at any time in the app's settings panel.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be published here with the updated date above.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact: cocmaxbasemonitor@gmail.com
