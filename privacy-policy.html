MaxBase - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

1. Data Collection and Use

MaxBase does not collect or transmit any personal data to any external servers.

  • Only in-game player tags, names, and preferences (like theme or notification settings) are stored.
  • All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage.
  • This data is used solely to display and personalize your app experience.

2. Data Sharing

MaxBase does not share any user data with third parties. Your account data remains on your device at all times.

3. Data Deletion

You can delete any stored data anytime by removing accounts or clearing the app storage from your device settings.

This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
